About Us

"Life Of chai is A Place Where Good vibes & Good Chai Collide."

LOC - Life Of Chai in Tiranga Square, Nagpur, is a popular, highly-rated tea lounge known for premium chai, coffee, and snacks like Bun Maska. Located in the Raghuji Nagar/Tiranga Chowk area, it offers a youthful, cozy atmosphere and is part of a 19-outlet chain specializing in tea-focused, budget-friendly quick bites.

Atmosphere & Specialty: Known for a lively atmosphere, open kitchen, and a diverse menu focusing on chai (Adrak, Elaichi), premium coffee, shakes, and fast food.

Location: Situated in Tiranga Square, Manewada Road, which is a major, accessible area in Nagpur.

Popular Menu Items: Customers frequently enjoy Bun Maska, Vada Pav, cheesy Maggi, and varied tea options.

Operating Hours: Usually open from morning until late night, often 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM or later.

Reputation: Recognized as a top tea spot in the area with high customer satisfaction for cleanliness and ambiance.
